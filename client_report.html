General Experience and Reflections

The most challenging part of the project was coordinating with other developers. I feel this makes it difficult to get the project done overall. Sometimes in some projects you don't really need to coordinate, but I feel like if we communicated more and thorougly the project would have been completed in a more optimize way.

My favorite part of this project is working in a team, it's so cool seeing the ideas come to life through our collective efforts. Especially using Github, working on a team was probably my favorite part of the project even when sometimes I would stress about the projects being finished.

What I would do differently if I were to do the project again is to communicate more with my team members. Maybe even meet somewhere so we can get it done.

Overall, I would like to develop my skills more and experience when working around with CSS and HTML. Until then I would feel like more prepared to work in real projects and experiences with people. This was a fun thing to try, and I am interested in these types of projects. The next time I do one, I'll be more prepared and ready to work with a team.
